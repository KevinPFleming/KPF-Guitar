Welcome to KPFMusic!

Sharing the Gift of Music is my Way of Life

Having been involved in music for 3 decades, teaching and sharing the gift of music has become the love of my life, especially with kids. Music is great for kids in many ways including: brain and intelligence development, learning to work as a team with others, and enjoying the enrichment and beauty that music offers.
